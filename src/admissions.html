Eligibility

Admission Criteria

Meet the following academic requirements to be eligible for admission to our programs.

BS Computer Science

📋

Intermediate / A-Levels

Minimum 50% marks in Intermediate with Pre-Engineering or Computer Science subjects from a recognized board.

📐

Mathematics Required

Mathematics as a compulsory subject at intermediate level is mandatory for the BS program.

📝

Entry Test

Valid NTS / COMSATS Aptitude Test score. Minimum 50% required. Tests held twice annually.

🎯

Merit Criteria

Merit = 50% Test + 25% Matric + 25% Intermediate. Final merit list published on official portal.

MS / PhD Computer Science

🎓

BS Degree (MS)

Minimum CGPA 2.5/4.0 in BS Computer Science, IT, Software Engineering, or related field from HEC-recognized university.

🎓

MS Degree (PhD)

Minimum CGPA 3.0/4.0 in MS or 18-year education in relevant field. Research proposal required.

📝

GAT Test

Valid GAT (General) score of 50+ for MS. GAT (Subject) score of 60+ for PhD admission.

🗣️

Interview

Shortlisted candidates must appear for an in-person or online interview with the departmental committee.

Application Process

How to Apply

Follow these steps to complete your application for admission to COMSATS CS Department.

01

Register Online

Create an account on the COMSATS admissions portal at admission.comsats.edu.pk

02

Fill Application Form

Complete the online application form with academic history and personal information.

03

Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of transcripts, ID, photos, and test score certificates.

04

Pay Application Fee

Pay Rs. 1,500 application fee via bank challan, JazzCash, or online banking.

05

Appear for Test

Attend the departmental aptitude test or submit valid NTS/GAT score.

06

Check Merit List

Merit lists are published on the portal. Selected students confirm enrollment and pay dues.
